BalanceFit

Saturday - Upper Body Strength

  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Sunday - Lower Body Strength
  • Squats: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Hamstring Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
Monday - High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)
  • 30 seconds of sprinting or high-intensity cardio (e.g., rowing)
  • 30 seconds of rest or low-intensity cardio
  • Circuit 1: Burpees, Mountain Climbers, Jumping Jacks (3 rounds, 30 seconds each)
Tuesday - Full Body Functional Training
  • Kettlebell Swings: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • TRX Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Medicine Ball Slams: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Box Jumps: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank with Shoulder Taps: 3 sets of 20 taps
Wednesday - Cardio and Core
  • 20 minutes of steady-state cardio (e.g., running or cycling)
  • Circuit 1: Bicycle Crunches, Russian Twists, Leg Raises (3 rounds, 15 reps each)
  • Circuit 2: Plank, Side Plank (each side) (3 rounds, 30 seconds each)
Thurday - Flexibility and Recovery
  • Yoga or Pilates session focusing on flexibility and relaxation (30-45 minutes)
  • Foam Rolling: 10-15 minutes on major muscle groups
  • Deep breathing and relaxation exercises
